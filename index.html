To use Ghost Writer, select a key and tonality at the top of the page. Next, click on the chord buttons in the middle of the page to add chords to your progression. Or, click "I'm Feeling Lucky", and Ghost Writer will pick chords for you. To delete a chord from your progression, simply click on that chord. Lastly, click the play button on the left side of the page to hear the chords you've selected.

Click the symbol next to some chord buttons to access additional options for that chord.

Click the symbol next to the chord buttons to see a guitar fingering chart for that chord.

Ghost Writer is a tool for both aspiring and experienced songwriters. Here are just a few of the ways that you can use it to improve your craft:

  • Play back chord progressions when you're on the go and don't have a guitar handy
  • Fix that one chord in a progression that just doesn't seem to fit
  • Break out of a songwriting rut by trying chord progressions that you may not have used before
  • Learn how to play the most widely used chords in popular music

Ghost Writer uses probability statistics from a research study conducted by the good folks at HookTheory. Check out their website for a thorough explanation of the thought process behind their study.
